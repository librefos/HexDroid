HexDroid is a modern, lightweight IRC client for Android that lets you connect to your favourite IRC networks, join channels, chat with friends, and stay up to date with a clean, fast Material Design UI.

Features:

* Multiple network support - connect to several IRC networks simultaneously
* IRCv3 capabilities - message-tags, server-time, echo-message, SASL authentication, and more
* TLS/SSL support - secure connections with optional client certificates
* Auto-connect and auto-join - automatically connect to networks and join channels on startup
* SASL authentication - PLAIN, EXTERNAL (client cert), and SCRAM-SHA-256 mechanisms
* Post-connect commands - run custom commands after connecting
* Character encoding support - auto-detect or manually set encoding (UTF-8, windows-1251, etc.)
* Nick highlighting and notifications - get notified when someone mentions you
* Channel and private message logging
* Ignore list - filter out unwanted messages
* Lag indicator - monitor connection quality
* Clean Material Design 3 interface

HexDroid is free and open source software, licensed under GPLv3.
